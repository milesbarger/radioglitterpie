The Spinitron advantage

Spinitron offers an online playlist management service specifically designed for non-commercial community and educational radio stations. We provide the database, application, servers, technical and customer service, and protection of your data. There is no software to install – all you need is web access.

Sophisticated features for data entry and manipulation offer many benefits for DJs and stations. But when playlist are online in real-time, benefits flow also to listeners and others.

For radio stations

  • Log and publish playlists online
  • Track all music played in a structured database
  • Prepare reports of use for ASCAP, BMI, SESAC and SoundExchange
  • Generate statistical reports such as Top-30-type charts
  • Manage shows, program schedule and DJs
  • Integrate Spinitron into your station's web site

For DJs

  • Put your playlist online in real-time
  • Connect with listeners interested in the music you play
  • Fast data entry reusing previously logged data to save typing
  • Enter playlists before, during or after your show from anywhere on the internet
  • Record the artistry of your programming for posterity
  • Help bands and artists by publishing your spins online

For listeners

  • Find out about music you hear on the radio
  • Discover music by exploring playlists by the same and other DJs, disks by the same artist or on the same label
  • Browse all the artists, disks, songs and labels logged by a station
  • Find a station's program schedule
  • RSS feeds of spins on a station

For artists and record labels

  • Browse your disks logged on Spinitron
  • Find who spun your music and when
  • Spinitron helps listeners find details of you and your music online
  • When you ask them, stations can find out if your music has been played and how often, by whom, etc.
  • NOTE: please do not send Spinitron promotional material. We are not a radio station!
